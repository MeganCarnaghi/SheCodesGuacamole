What is guacamole?

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip, spread, or salad, first developed in Mexico. In addition to its use in modern Mexican cuisine, it has become part of international and American cuisine as a dip, condiment and salad ingredient.

World's greatest guacamole recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocadoes - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground pepper

Directions

  1. Step 1: In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.
  2. Step 2: Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in pepper.
  3. Step 3: Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
